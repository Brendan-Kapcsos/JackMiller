Meet Jack

Jack Miller is a longtime Milton resident who has built his family and career right here in our community. A financial planner with a local firm in Crabapple, Jack serves on the board of the Milton First Responders Foundation, supports the Milton Historical Society, and is active in the Rotary Club of Alpharetta.

Jack’s service has been recognized by the City of Milton with the Silver Horseshoe of Service Award. He is also the Founder and President of the North Fulton Young Republicans.

Jack lives in Milton with his wife, Taylor, his high school sweetheart. Both are proud alumni of Milton High School and the University of Georgia.
